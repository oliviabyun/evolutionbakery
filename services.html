Evolution Bakery is a custom bakery specializing in cakes for all occasions. We have been in business for nearly 10 years! Please see below for general information about the products and services we offer. For more detailed information on our products, please email us at evolutionbakery@gmail.com.

Our business hours are Monday-Saturday 9am-5pm. We are closed to the public on Sundays.

We are able to take orders through our order form, phone, and in-person. For wedding cake design consultations and tastings, please call us to arrange.

We offer cakes for all occasions including birthdays, baby and bridal showers, corporate events, holidays, and any else you would like to celebrate. No matter the event you are planning, we can help create a beautiful and custom cake that will taste as good as it looks. Please contact us by phone with at least 7-10 days notice to place an order, or to receive a price quote send us an email with at least 2 weeks notice. We do not take orders through email.
